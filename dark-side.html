Welcome to the Dark Side

The Sith embrace the Dark Side of the Force, seeking power, control, and dominance over the galaxy. They harness their emotions—especially fear, anger, and hatred—to achieve greater strength and influence.

The Dark Side is a path filled with temptation and corruption, offering immense power but at a devastating cost. Those who choose this path must navigate the seductive nature of the Dark Side, often leading to a loss of self and morality.
