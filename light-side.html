Jedi Ranks

Rank Description Famous Jedi
Padawan A Jedi apprentice under the tutelage of a Jedi Knight or Master. Anakin Skywalker, Ahsoka Tano
Jedi Knight A full-fledged Jedi who has completed their training and trials. Obi-Wan Kenobi, Qui-Gon Jinn
Jedi Master A wise and experienced Jedi Knight, often taking on apprentices. Yoda, Mace Windu
Jedi Grand Master The highest rank, representing the leader of the Jedi Order. Yoda, Luke Skywalker
